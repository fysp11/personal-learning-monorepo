# Flight Planning: São Paulo ↔ Amsterdam

## Outbound: São Paulo (GRU) → Amsterdam (AMS)

### Routes

| Option | Airline | Stops | Duration | Typical Price (EUR) |
|--------|---------|-------|----------|-------------------|
| Direct | KLM | 0 | ~11h 50m | €700–1,200 |
| One-stop | TAP (Portugal) | LIS | ~13–14h | €500–900 |
| One-stop | Air France | CDG | ~14–15h | €600–1,000 |
| One-stop | LATAM | various | ~13–18h | €600–1,100 |
| One-stop | Iberia | MAD | ~14–16h | €550–950 |

### Price Tips
- **Best timing:** Book 2–3 months ahead for economy
- **Cheapest:** TAP via Lisbon often under €600
- **KLM direct:** More reliable pricing, ~€800 average
- **Skyscanner / Google Flights:** Compare all routes

### For 2 People
- Book together for better pricing
- Check "family" or "duo" discounts on some airlines
- Most include 1 checked bag (23kg) per person
- Some airlines charge extra for 2nd bag — check before booking

## Return: Amsterdam (AMS) → São Paulo (GRU)

### Routes (same options reversed)
- KLM direct: ~11h 50m
- TAP via LIS, Air France via CDG, etc.

### Price Ranges (2024–2025)
- Economy: €600–1,200 per person
- Peak season (July–August, December): €1,000–1,500

### Tips
- If planning regular visits, look into "open-jaw" tickets (AMS→GRU, GIG→AMS)
- KLM often runs deals for NL-Brazil routes
- Consider Star Alliance (LATAM + KLM) for mileage

## General Flight Advice

### Booking Platforms
- **Skyscanner** — best for multi-airline comparison
- **Google Flights** — flexible date search, price graphs
- **Kayak** — good for routing options
- **Airline direct** — sometimes cheaper, always check KLM.com, TAP.com

### Baggage
- Most economy tickets: 1 checked bag (23kg) + carry-on
- Work laptop usually counts as carry-on
- Some airlines (low-cost legs) charge for checked bag — read the fine print

### Travel Insurance
- Consider coverage for: flight delays, luggage loss, trip interruption
- Credit cards sometimes include travel insurance — check your card

### Best Months to Fly
- **Cheapest:** March–May, September–November
- **Most expensive:** July–August (Dutch summer holidays), December
- **Shoulder:** January–February, June — moderate prices
