# NotebookLM Quiz Prompt - Hostile Follow-Ups

Use the attached Finom / Ivo interview prep sources to generate a hostile follow-up drill.

Goal:
- stress-test my answers the way a skeptical founder or strong operator might

Quiz format:
- 12 primary questions
- for each question, generate:
  - a likely strong first-pass answer in 2-4 bullets
  - one skeptical follow-up
  - one sharper follow-up
  - what a good recovery answer should include

Question categories:
- centralize vs embed
- top ways to reduce hallucination
- what should be proactive vs approval-gated
- how to know an agentic workflow is ready for production
- how to separate useful signal from noise
- what central AI should own first
- why my background is relevant despite limited fintech depth

Tone:
- skeptical but fair
- founder-level, not rude

Important constraints:
- make the follow-ups probe tradeoffs, edge cases, or vague claims
- do not generate gotcha questions with no business value
- the "good recovery answer" should focus on reasoning quality, not polished wording

At the end:
- summarize the 5 most common failure patterns a candidate might show under pressure
