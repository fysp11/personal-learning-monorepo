# NotebookLM Flashcards Prompt - Hostile Follow-Ups

Use the attached Finom / Ivo interview prep sources to generate a flashcard deck for hostile follow-ups.

Goal:
- help me stay composed when an interviewer challenges an answer

Flashcard format:
- 20 cards total
- front side: skeptical follow-up question
- back side:
  - what the interviewer is really testing
  - what a strong recovery answer should include

Coverage:
- "How do you stop central AI from becoming a bottleneck?"
- "Why isn't human review enough?"
- "Why not just buy everything?"
- "How do you know you're not creating alert spam?"
- "Why should I trust your judgment without deep fintech experience?"
- "What would make this fail?"
- "What would you not automate?"

Important constraints:
- make the follow-ups realistic and business-relevant
- do not create gotcha questions with no decision value
- keep the answers focused on reasoning quality, not polished script writing

At the end:
- cluster the cards into "org-design pressure", "risk pressure", and "credibility pressure"
