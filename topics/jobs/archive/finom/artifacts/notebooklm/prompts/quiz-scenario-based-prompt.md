# NotebookLM Quiz Prompt - Scenario Based

Use the attached Finom / Ivo interview prep sources to create a scenario-based quiz.

Goal:
- test whether I can apply the prep material to realistic interview situations, not just recall it

Quiz format:
- 10 scenarios
- each scenario should be 3-6 sentences
- after each scenario, ask 2 questions:
  - what is the strongest answer direction?
  - what is the key mistake to avoid?
- provide a model answer after all scenarios

Scenario themes:
- central AI team vs product squads
- AI accounting trust and approval design
- rollout of proactive workflows
- build vs buy tradeoffs
- evaluation / observability / failure handling
- signal vs noise in proactive alerts

Difficulty:
- medium to hard
- assume I need to reason under ambiguity

Important constraints:
- make the scenarios feel like real founder or product-operator prompts
- avoid generic ML theory questions
- force tradeoffs, not one obviously correct answer

At the end:
- rank the scenarios from easiest to hardest
- explain which ones are closest to what Ivo is most likely to care about
