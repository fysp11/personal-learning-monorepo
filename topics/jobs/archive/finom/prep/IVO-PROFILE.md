# Ivo Profile Research

Saved: 2026-04-06

## High-Confidence Signals

### Finom role history
- Multiple public sources identify `Ivo Dimitrov` as a senior Finom founder/product leader.
- A January 17, 2025 FinTech Futures article says Finom hired Kristjan Kaar as new CPO and that Kaar would succeed `Finom co-founder Ivo Dimitrov`, who had spent more than five years as CPO and was moving into the role of `Chief Venture Officer`.
- Finextra coverage from February 25, 2025 and September 16, 2025 quotes Ivo as `Chief Venture Officer` and, in the later article, as `Chief Venture Officer and co-founder of Finom`.
- Recruiter Samuel's message says Ivo runs a separate `central AI team`.

### Product / strategy orientation
- Older public coverage clearly frames Ivo as a product leader.
- A September 18, 2023 interview describes him as `co-founder and CPO of Finom`.
- In that interview he talks about:
- building a native SME banking product rather than adapting consumer banking
- strong mobile usage
- usability as a strategic differentiator
- future financial copilots using AI / ML
- integrating business accounts with the wider operating stack used by SMEs

### Likely current scope
- Based on the recruiter message plus 2025 press coverage, the best working assumption is:
- he started as a founding product leader / CPO
- he later moved into a broader venture / AI-oriented role
- he is now involved in Finom's more central AI initiatives rather than only standard product management

## Lower-Confidence / Mixed Signals

### Current title is inconsistent across public sources
- FinTech Futures (January 2025) says `Chief Venture Officer`.
- Finextra (February and September 2025) also calls him `Chief Venture Officer`.
- The Org currently lists him as `Chief AI Officer`, and says he has `12 years in design and product management` and `6+ years in fintech`, based in Berlin.

Best interpretation:
- `Chief Venture Officer` appears better supported by dated 2025 press coverage.
- `Chief AI Officer` may reflect a more recent title update, or it may be an unverified org-chart label.
- For interview prep, it is safest to treat him as a co-founder-level leader currently associated with central AI / venture work rather than rely on one exact title.

### Founder status is also inconsistent
- Finom's public homepage / FAQ currently lists four founders: `Andrey Petrov`, `Oleg Laguta`, `Konstantin Stiskin`, and `Yakov Novikov`.
- However, recruiter Samuel referred to Ivo as `one of the co-founders`, and multiple third-party/public articles also call him a co-founder.

Best interpretation:
- Internally and in external media, he is treated as a co-founder-level figure.
- Finom's own public founder list does not currently include him, so this is worth treating carefully if the topic comes up.

## Working Profile For Interview Prep

The most defensible profile is:

`Ivo Dimitrov` is a Berlin-based senior Finom leader with a strong product background who appears to have spent the company's first years as CPO and then shifted into a broader venture / AI mandate. He is publicly associated with Finom's AI Accounting push and is likely one of the key people shaping how AI becomes a central capability across the company.

## What This Suggests About His Angle

You should expect him to care about:
- product leverage across the company, not just one squad
- user-centered AI, not novelty AI
- central capability building
- reusable AI patterns and quality controls
- how AI changes the SME financial operating model
- practical business outcomes over technical theater

## Best Conversation Implications

### Avoid
