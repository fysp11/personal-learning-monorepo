# Ivo / Finom Web Research

Saved: 2026-04-06

## Bottom line

The strongest public read is:
- Ivo is likely to interview as a founder-level product operator, not a narrow engineering manager.
- Finom is publicly framing AI as embedded financial operations, especially around accounting, documents, and controlled automation.
- The most plausible interview paths are:
  - `Direction A`: central AI mandate, org design, reusable capability layers, build-vs-buy
  - `Direction B`: AI accounting quality, trust, thresholds, human review, rollout discipline

## Scope of this note

This note is an external cross-check for the second-round prep. It is meant to answer:
- what public signals say about Ivo's background
- what Finom is publicly saying about AI and accounting
- what interview patterns are common for a senior applied AI role

It is not meant to prove one exact current title with certainty.

## 1. Role and company signals

### Signal

Finom's public Senior AI Engineer role is broad, product-facing, and explicitly production-heavy.

### Evidence

The public job description says the role is expected to:
- build AI systems across onboarding, customer support, AI accounting, fraud / risk, document understanding, and internal automation
- own the full lifecycle from problem framing through evaluation, deployment, monitoring, and iteration
- contribute to AI platform and tooling decisions that improve reuse and consistency across teams
- raise the technical bar for how Finom builds and operates AI systems

### Why it matters for interview 2

This makes both of these interview directions plausible:
- a company-level leverage conversation
- a concrete risk / workflow-quality conversation

It also supports the idea that Ivo may test both product judgment and operating rigor in the same interview.

## 6. Cross-industry interview pattern check

### Signal

Senior applied AI interviews increasingly combine system design, business framing, and production operations.

### Evidence

Generic ML / applied AI interview guidance emphasizes:
- turning business problems into ML / AI systems
- system design under production constraints
- evaluation and monitoring
- safety / harmful-output prevention
- inference infrastructure and tradeoffs

Public interview-question aggregators for applied AI roles also show a mix of:
- shipped work discussion
- architecture tradeoffs
- code / implementation fluency
- repo or portfolio evidence
- product / goal framing

### Why it matters for interview 2

Even if Ivo is not the final technical evaluator, it is normal for him to test whether your stories reflect real ownership rather than AI vocabulary.
