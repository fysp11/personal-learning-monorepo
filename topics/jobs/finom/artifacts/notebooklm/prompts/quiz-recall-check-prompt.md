# NotebookLM Quiz Prompt - Recall Check

Use the attached Finom / Ivo interview prep sources to generate a compact recall quiz.

Goal:
- test whether I can remember the key facts, themes, and distinctions without notes

Quiz format:
- 20 questions total
- mix of short answer and multiple choice
- keep questions concise
- after all questions, provide an answer key with 1-2 sentence explanations

Focus areas:
- Finom company and role context
- who Ivo likely is and what lens he brings
- the two main interview paths
- central AI vs product squad boundaries
- trust / control / evaluation themes
- top candidate questions to ask Ivo

Difficulty:
- medium
- not trivia-heavy
- prioritize important distinctions over obscure details

Important constraints:
- do not ask questions that depend on exact wording from a source
- test conceptual retention, not memorization of phrasing
- include at least 5 questions that force me to distinguish between two similar ideas

At the end:
- identify the 3 areas where a weak score would be most dangerous in the actual interview
