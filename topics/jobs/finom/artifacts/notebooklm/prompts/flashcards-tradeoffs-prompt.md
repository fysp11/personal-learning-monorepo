# NotebookLM Flashcards Prompt - Tradeoffs

Use the attached Finom / Ivo interview prep sources to generate a flashcard deck focused on tradeoffs.

Goal:
- train me to speak in defensible tradeoffs instead of generic best practices

Flashcard format:
- 24 cards total
- front side: tradeoff prompt
- back side: strongest answer structure with 2-5 bullets

Coverage:
- centralize vs embed
- automation vs approval
- build vs buy
- speed vs control
- proactive alerts vs noise
- generality vs workflow-specific design
- product-team autonomy vs central standards
- benchmark quality vs end-to-end workflow quality

Important constraints:
- every card must include both sides of the tradeoff
- avoid one-sided "correct answer" phrasing
- answers should explain what factors shift the decision

At the end:
- identify the 5 tradeoffs most likely to come up with Ivo
