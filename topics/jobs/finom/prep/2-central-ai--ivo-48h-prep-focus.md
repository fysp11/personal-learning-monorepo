## What To Focus On In The Next 48 Hours

### 1. Founder-level narrative
Be able to explain clearly:
- who you are in one tight paragraph
- why Finom specifically
- why you fit a central-AI / cross-company mandate
- why your background is more relevant than a pure research profile

Target:
- one 30-second version
- one 2-minute version

### 2. Central AI vs product squads
This is probably the most interview-specific topic for Ivo.

Be ready to explain:
- what should be centralized
- what should remain embedded in product teams
- how to avoid duplicate AI efforts across squads
- how a central AI team enables without becoming a bottleneck

Target:
- one clear opinionated answer with examples

### 3. Fintech-risk judgment
You do not need fake fintech expertise, but you do need credible judgment about:
- correctness-sensitive workflows
- confidence thresholds
- human review
- rollback / safe-failure design
- failure costs in financial operations

Target:
- 2 to 3 examples of how you would raise the bar in finance/accounting AI systems

### 4. Execution stories under pressure
Have your best stories ready in compact form:
- production agent systems
- document-heavy workflows
- evaluation / observability
- infrastructure ownership
- high-ambiguity delivery

Target:
- 3 stories
- each with problem, approach, tradeoff, result, and why it matters to Finom

### 5. Questions for Ivo
This interview will go better if your questions show platform and org judgment, not just curiosity.

Focus your questions on:
- mandate of the central AI team
- boundary between central AI and product engineering
- what reusable AI capability means at Finom
- whether this role is still evolving because the org model is still evolving

Target:
- top 5 questions
- top 2 if time is short

## Recommended Allocation

If the next 48 hours are limited, the best allocation is probably:

- `30%` narrative and positioning
- `25%` central AI vs product squad thinking
- `20%` story rehearsal
- `15%` fintech / risk / governance framing
- `10%` question prep

With Dmitry, the strongest proof point was:

With Ivo, the stronger proof point is:

That means your prep should shift slightly upward in altitude:
