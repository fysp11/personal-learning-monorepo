## Housing in Amsterdam

### Key areas to explore
- **Amsterdam Zuid / Buitenveldert** — near business districts, quieter
- **Amsterdam Oud-Zuid** — family-friendly, good transport
- **Amsterdam West (De Baarsjes, Bos en Lommer)** — more affordable, up-and-coming
- **Amsterdam Noord** — newer developments, ferry/metro to central
- **Amstelveen** — suburban, near Schiphol, good for expats
- **Diemen / Diemen-Zuid** — affordable, metro to city center
- **Haarlem** — 15 min train, quieter city feel

### Housing platforms
- [funda.nl](https://funda.nl) — main Dutch property portal (buy + rent)
- [pararius.nl](https://pararius.nl) — rental listings
- [kamernet.nl](https://kamernet.nl) — rooms and studios
- [housinganywhere.com](https://housinganywhere.com) — expat-friendly rentals
- [expathousing.nl](https://expathousing.nl) — expat-focused
- Facebook groups: "Expats in Amsterdam Housing", "Amsterdam Apartments for Rent"

### Budget considerations
- Studio: ~€1,200–1,800/month
- 1-bedroom apartment: ~€1,500–2,500/month
- 2-bedroom apartment: ~€2,000–3,500/month
- Amsterdam housing market is tight — start early, expect competition

## Practical Relocation Checklist

### Before moving
- [ ] Register with municipality (gemeente) within 5 days of arriving — **required**
- [ ] Get BSN (Burger Service Nummer) — issued at municipality registration
- [ ] Open Dutch bank account (ING, ABN AMRO, Rabobank, bunq)
- [ ] Get Dutch health insurance (basisverzekering) — **mandatory within 4 months**
- [ ] Get OV-chipkaart for public transport
- [ ] Arrange housing (temporary first, then permanent)
- [ ] Set up mail forwarding from current address

### After arriving
- [ ] Register address at gemeente
- [ ] Set up utilities (internet, electricity, gas)
- [ ] Get Dutch phone number (or keep current + switch to EU plan)
- [ ] Find a huisarts (GP) — register with a local practice
- [ ] Explore neighborhoods in person before committing to long-term housing

## Transport
- **OV-chipkaart** — reloadable card for trains, metro, trams, buses
- **NS** — national rail (Amsterdam Centraal to Schiphol: 15 min)
- **GVB** — Amsterdam public transport (metro, tram, bus)
- **Cycling** — primary mode for locals. Buy a bike within the first week.
- **Delphyr office location** — confirm commute options from preferred neighborhoods
