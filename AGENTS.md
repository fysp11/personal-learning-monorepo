# Claude Instructions

## General Instructions
Keep findings, research, insights and other relevant information persisted in this folder, under grouped/organized sub-folders, following @README.md.

## Topic: Jobs (`topics/jobs/`)
Job search preparation workspace. Each company gets its own subdirectory under `topics/jobs/<company>/`.

### Per-Company Structure
- `README.md` — company overview, role details, interview timeline
- `CLAUDE.md` — company-specific Claude instructions
- `AGENTS.md` — company-specific agent instructions
- Research reports, cheatsheets, and prep materials
- `interviews/` — captured materials and source records
